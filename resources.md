# Trusted Free AI Tools - Course Handout

This list focuses on high-quality tools available in the Netherlands that do not require a paid subscription for basic use.

## 1. General Assistants (Text & Logic)

- **ChatGPT (OpenAI):** [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) - The industry standard.
- **Claude (Anthropic):** [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) - Excellent for writing and nuance.
- **Gemini (Google):** [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com) - Best for Google ecosystem integration.
- **Perplexity AI:** [perplexity.ai](https://perplexity.ai) - The best search engine replacement (cites sources).

## 2. Creativity & Design

- **OpenArt:** [openart.ai](https://openart.ai) - Powerful image generation and editing.
- **Canva Magic Studio:** [canva.com](https://canva.com) - AI-assisted graphic design.
- **Adobe Firefly:** [firefly.adobe.com](https://firefly.adobe.com) - High-quality image generation.

## 3. Productivity & Work

- **ElevenLabs:** [elevenlabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io) - The gold standard for text-to-speech.
- **Otter.ai:** [otter.ai](https://otter.ai) - Meeting transcription and notes.
- **DeepL:** [deepl.com](https://deepl.com) - Superior AI-powered translation.

## 4. Advanced / Coding

- **Antigravity / Windsurf:** For intelligent code editing and file management.
- **Figma Weave:** For UI/UX designers looking to speed up workflows.

---
**Tip:** Always verify the results of AI-generated content, especially for facts, dates, and mathematical calculations!
