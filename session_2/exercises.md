# Exercise: Choose Your Task

Choose one of the following tasks and try to complete it using at least two different models (e.g., ChatGPT and Claude) to compare the results.

### Option A: The Creative Translator

Take a poem or a very complex technical text in another language and ask the AI to translate it while maintaining the style or simplifying it for a 10-year-old child.

### Option B: The Idea Debugger

Describe a problem you have (e.g., "I want to organize a 3-day trip to Utrecht") and ask the AI to find 3 weak points in your plan and suggest improvements.

### Option C: The Data Analyst

Copy and paste a series of data (e.g., a table of schedules or prices) and ask the AI to extract statistics or format them in a different way.

### Option D: The Pair Challenge (Rewriting)

Find a partner. One of you writes a "bad" (vague) prompt (e.g., "Tell me about food"). The other must rewrite it into a "master" prompt (e.g., "Give me a recipe for a traditional Dutch meal that can be prepared in 30 minutes, including a shopping list for 2 people").
Compare the two results.

---
**Goal:** Note if the AI "hallucinates" details (e.g., trains that don't exist) and try to correct the course by providing more context.
