## 1. History in Brief (Essential Timeline)

- **1950:** Alan Turing and the "Turing Test."
- **1956:** The Dartmouth Conference (birth of the term "Artificial Intelligence").
- **AI Winters:** Periods of stagnation in research.
- **2012:** The Deep Learning revolution (AlexNet).
- **2017:** The paper "Attention is All You Need" (birth of Transformers).
- **2022:** The explosion of ChatGPT.

## 2. Keywords (Glossary)

- **LLM (Large Language Model):** The textual "engine."
- **Prompt:** The instruction we give to the AI.
- **Tokens:** The "syllables" processed by the AI.
- **Fine-tuning:** Specific training on a dataset.
- **GenAI:** Generative AI (AI that creates content).
