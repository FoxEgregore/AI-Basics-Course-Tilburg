# The Grand Laboratory (Sessions 3 & 4)

Two sessions entirely dedicated to creating something of your own.

---

## Session 3: Planning and Launch (90 Minutes)

- **00-10 min:** Quick recap: the most common prompt mistakes.
- **10-30 min:** **Mini-showcase:** Discovering more tools (Windsurf, ElevenLabs, OpenArt).
- **30-45 min:** Theory: When NOT to use AI and how to fact-check.
- **45-90 min:** Project Selection and Session 1 work.

---

## Session 4: Finalization and Demo (90 Minutes)

- **00-60 min:** Project completion and final touches.
- **60-75 min:** Guided reflections on the process and obstacles encountered.
- **75-90 min:** "Flash Demos" (2 min per participant) and final wrap-up.

---

## 1. Tool Showcase (Session 3)

Beyond the chat window, we have specialized tools:

- **Windsurf / Antigravity:** Smart code and file management.
- **ElevenLabs:** Professional voice-to-text and translation.
- **OpenArt / Figma Weave:** AI-powered design and image generation.

## 2. The Pilot’s Vet: When Not to use AI

- **Fact-Checking:** AI doesn't have a concept of "truth." Always verify names, dates, and numbers.
- **Privacy:** Never use AI for sensitive internal documents or personal passwords.
- **The Human Touch:** Use AI for the *draft*, not the *final* version of emotional or highly personal messages.

## 3. Phase 1: Project Selection

Each participant chooses a goal. Examples:

- Create a small personal website.
- Write a series of illustrated stories (also using image generators).
- Create an automation to handle emails or documents.
- Analyze a dataset from your own work.

**Day 3 Tasks:**

1. Goal definition.
2. Project outline (What do I need to do first? What next?).
3. Start implementation with AI.

## Phase 2: Finalization and Reflections (Session 4)

Continue the work started in the previous session.

**Day 4 Tasks:**

1. Project closure (or a working module).
2. **Review:** What was easy? What was frustrating?
3. **Presentation:** 2 minutes to show others what came out of the "laboratory."

## Final Reflections

- How will your way of working change after these 4 weeks?
- Which tool surprised you the most?
- Is AI a "magic wand" or a craftsman's tool?
